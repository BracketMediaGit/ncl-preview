At a time of rising political violence, threats against public officials, and increasing attempts to politicize law enforcement for partisan gain, The Trust & Legitimacy Project exists to reaffirm the true meaning of the rule of law: equal justice, constitutional accountability, and public safety free from political intimidation or extremism.
